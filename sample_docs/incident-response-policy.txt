Company Incident Response Policy

Version: 1.0

#Purpose#

This policy defines how security incidents must be reported and managed.

Requirements

1. All suspected security incidents must be reported to the Security Operations Centre (SOC).

2. Critical incidents must be escalated within 30 minutes.

3. Evidence must be preserved before remediation activities begin.

4. Customer data incidents require Security, Privacy and Legal review.

5. Root cause analysis must be completed after containment.

6. Lessons learned must be documented following major incidents.
