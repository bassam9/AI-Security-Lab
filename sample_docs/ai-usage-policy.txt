Company AI Usage Policy

Version: 1.0

#Purpose#
This policy defines the approved use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) systems within the organization.

Requirements

1. Confidential information must not be submitted to public AI services.

2. Customer payment card information, passwords, API keys, tokens, encryption keys and regulated personal information must never be submitted to public AI platforms.

3. Only approved enterprise AI platforms may process confidential information.

4. AI-generated content must be reviewed and approved by a human before business use.

5. Prompt injection testing must be completed before production deployment.

6. AI system activity should be logged for security monitoring and investigations.

7. All AI applications must follow the Secure AI Development Standard.
