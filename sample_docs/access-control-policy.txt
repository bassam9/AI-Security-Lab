Company Access Control Policy

Version: 1.0

#Purpose#

This policy defines user access requirements.

Requirements

1. Access must follow the principle of least privilege.

2. Multi-factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory for privileged accounts.

3. Administrative access requires manager approval.

4. Shared administrator accounts are prohibited.

5. User access reviews must be completed quarterly.

6. Dormant accounts must be disabled after 90 days.
